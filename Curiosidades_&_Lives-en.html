Trivia About Ado

From utaite scene to mainstream

Ado started out on the internet as an utaite, recording covers and taking part in Vocaloid culture. This online background helped her build a fanbase even before her official debut with “Usseewa” in 2020.

Striking voice & discreet visual identity

One of Ado’s trademarks is the contrast between her extremely expressive voice and her choice to preserve her image, using illustrations and specific camera cuts in concerts and videos to keep the focus on the music.

Connection with the anime world

Her work on “One Piece Film Red”, giving voice to the character Uta, brought Ado even closer to the anime audience, consolidating her presence in events and discussions within otaku culture around the world.

Impact of “Usseewa”

The aggressive lyrics and confrontational tone of “Usseewa” sparked debates in the media and online, including discussions about language and generational identity, while the song became one of the biggest J‑Pop streaming hits of its time.
